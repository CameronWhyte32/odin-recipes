Chicken Satay Recipe

A simple yet elegant beef short ribs recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast
  • Satay Sauce
  • Peanut Butter
  • Rice
  1. Brown the chicken in a hot pan
  2. Add the peanut butter and fry gently
  3. Add the satay sauce and simmer for 20 minutes
  4. Meanwhile boil the rice until tender
  5. Spoon atop the cooked rice
