Beef Short Ribs Recipe

A simple yet elegant beef short ribs recipe

Ingredients

  • Beef ribs
  • Beef Stock
  • Carrots
  • Red Wine
  • Flour
  1. Brown the ribs in a hot pan
  2. Add the carrots
  3. Add the wine and simmer to reduce
  4. Make a roux with the flour and add to the mix
  5. Add the beef stock and simmer for 3 hours
