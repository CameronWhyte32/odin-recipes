Steak and Ale Pie

A simple yet elegant beef short ribs recipe

Ingredients

  • Stewing steak
  • Beef Stock
  • Carrots
  • Beer, ale
  • Flour
  • Shortcrust pastry
  1. Brown the beef in a hot pan
  2. Add the carrots
  3. Add the beef and simmer to reduce
  4. Make a roux with the flour and add to the mix
  5. Add the beef stock and simmer for 3 hours
  6. Cool the filling then spoon into a pie dish and top with pastry
  7. Cook for 1 hour at 180C fan
